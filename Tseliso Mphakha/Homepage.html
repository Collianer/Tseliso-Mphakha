WHO ARE WE

Hi! We are Fields of Dreams, a small farm
dedicated to providing the best quality produce
to our local community.

WHAT WE DO

We are very passionate to produce organic products.
We grow variety of fruits and vegetables, including
tomatoes, potatoes, peppers and others

WHY US

We believe in the importance of eating fresh,
locally grown food, and we are committed to supporting
our local community

WHERE ARE WE

Our Farm is located at Pitseng MPhosong in Leribe district.
We have offices at Maputsoe opposite to Lesotho Flour Mills
and in Butha Bothe Likila Complex near main road
